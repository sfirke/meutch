How Meutch works

A friendly, community-first way to share things with people you trust.

The basics

  1. Join circles. Circles are groups: a faith community, neighborhood, club, or just a group of friends. Circles make it easy to share with the people you know.
  2. List items you can lend. Add tools, kitchen gear, games, and more with a short description and an optional photo.
  3. Search when you need something. See who in your circles has what you need and how close they are to you.
  4. Request and arrange pickup. Agree with the owner on dates and pickup arrangements and any other details.

A quick example

Ana is hosting a graduation party and realizes she needs an extra cooler for drinks. She searches Meutch, finds a cooler listed by someone in her neighborhood circle, sends a request, and they agree on a pickup time the afternoon before the party. After the event, Ana returns the cooler and leaves a friendly thank-you message. The party was a success and no one had to buy - or store - a cooler.

Why we built Meutch

1-click ordering is convenient but costly; Meutch makes it easy to borrow from neighbors, saving money and reducing consumption.

Practical

Need it briefly? Borrow a cooler, ladder, or tent instead of buying and storing it.

Impact

Reduce waste and strengthen neighbor relationships through sharing.

Let's build community together

Together we can save money, slow consumerism, and strengthen neighbor relationships. Let's stop shopping and start sharing.
