How Meutch Works

Four simple steps to start sharing with your community.

1

Join a Circle

Circles are your communities: a neighborhood, club, faith group, or friend circle. They control who sees your stuff.

2

List Your Items

Add tools, kitchen gear, camping equipment — anything you'd lend to a friend. Got stuff to give away? List that too.

3

Find What You Need

Search for items nearby, or post a request to let your circles know what you're looking for.

4

Arrange the Handoff

Message the owner, agree on dates, and pick it up. Return it when you're done. That's it.

Why Meutch?

1-click ordering is convenient but costly. There's a better way.

Save Money & Space

Need a ladder for the weekend? A cooler for one party? Borrow it instead of buying and storing it forever.

Build Real Connections

We're surrounded by objects yet lack relationships with our neighbors. Sharing changes that.

Lighten Your Footprint

Every item borrowed is one less manufactured, shipped, and eventually landfilled. Small acts, big impact.
